A game for PSVita made at the IndieCade East '13 Sony PSM Game Jam. Cybrid-7X is a game about a robot girl and her garden overflowing with plant life. She is trying to find the perfect flower through cross-breeding, while also keeping everything organized in an attempt not to be overrun by the precocious plants popping up all around her.

Made by team donutGoku (Nina Freeman, Emmett Butler and Diego Garcia)
