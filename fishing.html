Hokuto no Huchen (Fist of the North Karp)

CLICK IN THE BOX ABOVE TO PLAY

Jump on the fish to help dad get it off the hook... but don't get fishslapped!

The second part in this series of childhood games is now up: My House My Rules.

Made @ Fishing Game Jam - Emmett Butler, Nina Freeman, audio by Deckman Coss

The inspiration for this game comes from a fishing trip I went on with my dad when I was a little girl. He caught a sunfish and thought it would be funny to swing it around on the line. At the end of his swing the sunfish smacked me right on the cheek.

This game was made using Flixel, Adobe Photoshop and GIMP. You can view the Github repository and our Fishing Jam Forum post.
